# Business Use Cases

- **Revenue:** lead routing, enrichment, abandoned carts, invoices, and renewals.
- **Operations:** approvals, appointments, documents, reports, inventory, and onboarding.
- **Customer experience:** ticket triage, SLA escalation, sentiment, reviews, and status notifications.
- **Marketing:** newsletters, attribution, content repurposing, social scheduling, and reports.
- **Engineering/data:** GitHub/Jira coordination, deployments, API monitoring, synchronization, backups, ETL, and quality checks.

Assign every automation an owner, success metric, SLO, fallback, and review date.
